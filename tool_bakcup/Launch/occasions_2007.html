The "Datapage" corporate day was colourful and bashful and our hearts were filled with joy and enthusiasm. Everyone had their breakfast after they were welcomed heartily by the "Sterling Resorts" group.
 
Pre-lunch Events
 

The day began with the cake cutting ceremony by our Operations Manager Nisha Rahul, followed by a group song by Prasanna, Henry, Aparna and Shobana.The various cultural events that took place were singing, skit performance, comedy show, Grand Master show, monoacting, poetry recitation and everyone participated with zeal.

Senthil Kumar, Sathish Kumar, Henry Prakash, Vijayabalan, Kannan, Shobana, Julia Bharathi, Gopinath and Rama actively participated in the singing competition. The Super Singer awards were bagged by Henry Prakash and Julia Bharathi. Henry Prakash, Aparna, Shobana, Sivagama Sundari, Usha, Madan and Rajkumar staged a humorous skit. The skit was pertaining to our industry and it made everyone laugh. The best skit performance was bagged by the Training and Copyediting department. A duet was sung by Prasanna and Aparna and it attracted everyone's attention.
 

"Just for Joke" was presented by Henry Prakash which made everyone laugh to their fullest. "Grand Master" was a wonderful show organised and presented by Rajkumar, Madhan, Sathish, Prasanna, Lalitha and Ramnath. The personalities within the company were identified during this game.

The next event was poetry recitation presented by Kannan, Raja and C. Lalitha. They proved their talents in a versatile manner. The monoact was performed by S. Lalitha which was meaningful and heart breaking.
 

Mr. &. Ms. Datapage

Mr.&.Ms.Datapage titles were conferred to Mohammed Parvez Ali and Nayagi, respectively. The voting took place last week and they were selected based on the maximum number of votes cast by the employees of Datapage.
 

Lunch

Lunch was a full-fledged course of vegetarian and non-vegetarian foods starting from soup to dessert.
 
Post-lunch Events
 

Datapage team was divided into five groups, namely Fire flies, Dream destroyers, Thunder racers, Miracle workers and Skull crackers. Each group consisted of about 27 members from different departments. The games were Blind Shapes, Group Jump, Blind Walk, Circle Dance and Musical Dance.
 

Blind Shapes

In this game, the group members were blindfolded and one member was asked to lead the group. Different geometrical shapes were formed by the group members as per the group leader's instruction. All the groups participated enthusiastically and it was a lively game. This game was won by Skull crackers as they formed a maximum number of three shapes within 5 minutes.
 

Group Jump

This was a challenging game for all the groups to prove their strength and calibre. Three members from each group jumped over the rope held by other two members of the group. Everyone participated energetically. This game was won by Dream destroyers with the maximum of 73 jumps within 5 minutes.
 

Blind Walk

The group members were blindfolded and chairs were kept as obstacles for this game. One member was asked to lead the group. The group members reached the destination successfully without touching the obstacles as per the group leader's instruction. It made everyone think while playing. This game was won by Skull crackers with a maximum of five persons directed successfully within 5 minutes.
 

Circle Dance

It was a very interesting game and the respective groups enjoyed by dancing in a very small circle.
 

Musical Dance

In this game, every individual participated and everyone danced several rounds as per the played music and the game was similar to musical chair. It was a real fun-loving game.
 
The overall champions of the day were Dream destroyers with the maximum number of points. Each member of the group received a marvellous prize as a token of appreciation.
 

Best Employee

The Best Employee award of the year was conferred to E. Venkatesan of Group 5 with a cash prize of Rs.5000/-. His speech was very encouraging and impressive.
 

The day ended with a vote of thanks delivered by Aparna.
 

This year's Corporate Day event was a milestone in the history of Datapage as it was well-organised by our HR Manager Mrs.Vijayalakshmi with the fullest participation by Datapage team.
