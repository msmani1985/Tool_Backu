The 6th anniversary of Datapage (India) Private Limited was commemorated with joyous celebrations from September 14, 2009 to September 19, 2009. The six days were filled with fun activities assuring people both joy and merriment. The details of the celebrations are as follows:

      

  • September 14, 2009 - We celebrated Surprise Gifts day where lots were picked up and the names of lucky winners were announced on an hourly basis till 6.30 PM.
  • September 15, 2009 was observed as "Charity and Posters Day" where the staff had contributed funds and clothes for a charity home. They had also displayed good posters on different social causes.
  • September 16, 2009 and September 17, 2009 were "Presentation Days" where each team gave a presentation on their achievements and accomplishments of last year.
  • September 18, 2009 was celebrated as "Crazy Dressing" day. All teams had dressed up as crazily as possible depending upon the theme they had selected.

 On September 19, 2009, we celebrated our "Corporate Day" at MGM Dizee world on the ECR, Chennai. The staff split themselves into groups to board the buses. All the buses were filled with high sprits since there was so much of music and dance by the staff.    

    

We reached MGM around 11.00 AM where we were welcomed with welcome drinks. From there all employees proceeded to enjoy the various rides such as boating, giant wheel, flying ship, spider spin, swimming pool, etc.

         

Around 1.30 PM we gathered in a place exclusively allocated for us to enjoy a combination of vegetarian and non-vegetarian buffet lunch.

 

The afternoon session started with the prize distribution ceremony. Ramnath, our Deputy Manager – Journals acted as compere starting off the session with a welcome speech. He thanked all teams for their active participation in the corporate week events and went on to invite key staff to distribute prizes for various events. Following are the teams which won the prizes for each event:

Presentation:  

  • I   - Group 5                            
  • II   - QC books                            
  • III  - Group 2                            

Posters:

  • I    - Copy Editing            
  • II   - QC            
  • III  - Training            

Crazy Dressing:

  • I   - Group 2
  • II   - Group 4
  • III  - IT

   

The employee of the year - Mr. Muthu Kumar E of EP division - was awarded the certificate and cash award. Some employees had also engaged the show by exhibiting their mimicry talents.

After the prize distribution, the staff disbursed for the rides yet again. In the evening we all gathered for tea and snacks. Around 5.30 pm, all of us proceeded back to the bus and left for office. On the whole all of us had a wonderful time throughout the day.
